About the Project

Veritatem is an academic proof-of-concept developed to demonstrate how AI-assisted clinical decision support systems could be structured.


Patient records are loaded from an external Excel dataset and visualized on the dashboard.


All probabilities, calculations and visualisations are illustrative and do not represent a validated clinical model.


The prototype was created for educational purposes and to explore concepts related to AI, healthcare and regulatory frameworks.
